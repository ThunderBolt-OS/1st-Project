Google Developer Student Clubs


Google Developer Student Clubs are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.


Facebook Developer Circles

br

Developer Circles are communities of innovators, where experienced developers receive free opportunities to strengthen their expertise in Facebook products. About the program Members receive coaching from technology experts, collaborate with like-minded peers, and take part in a range of building initiatives. Each Developer Circle is managed by a developer community leader who hosts local events, supports an online Facebook Group and shares the latest news on Facebook products. Aimed at advanced developers and creators, our tailored resources will help you take your software solutions to the next level.
